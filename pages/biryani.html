Chicken Biryani - Traditional Style

Indgridients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 200g chicken or vegetables (cubed)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tbsp oil/ghee
  • Fresh cilantro and mint (chopped)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice:

    Boil basmati rice with a pinch of salt until 70% done. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the base:

    Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, cardamom, cloves, and bay leaf. Add sliced onions and fry until golden.

  3. Add chicken or vegetables:

    Add chicken/veggies, tomatoes, yogurt, and biryani masala. Cook until meat is done and oil separates.

  4. Layer:

    In a large pot, layer half of the cooked rice, then the chicken/vegetable mixture, and top with the remaining rice.

  5. Steam:

    Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes.

  6. Garnish:

    Sprinkle with chopped cilantro, mint, and serve hot.
