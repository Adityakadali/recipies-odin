Butter Chicken - Indian Style

Ingredients:

  • 300g chicken (cubed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken:

    Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, and salt. Set aside for 30 minutes.

  2. Cook chicken:

    In a pan, melt butter and saut√© marinated chicken until cooked. Set aside.

  3. Prepare sauce:

    In the same pan, add chopped onions and cook until soft. Add tomato puree, chili powder, cumin, garam masala, and salt. Simmer for 10 minutes.

  4. Combine:

    Add cooked chicken to the sauce. Stir in cream and cook for 5 minutes.

  5. Serve:

    Garnish with cilantro and serve with naan or rice.
