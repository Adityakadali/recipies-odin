Cake - The Dessert

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven:

    Preheat to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan.

  2. Mix wet ingredients:

    Cream butter and sugar together. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.

  3. Mix dry ingredients:

    In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.

  4. Combine:

    Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with milk.

  5. Bake:

    Pour batter into the pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.

  6. Cool:

    Let the cake cool, then frost or serve as is.
